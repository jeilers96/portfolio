The Bashful Bookworm

The Bashful Bookworm is a short film I directed and co-wrote for the 2018 UAC 31 Day Film Project. "Bedtime Stories" was the theme of the competition. Judges required you to use a prop, character name, and line, and all footage and screenwriting needed to be completed in the 31-day timespan. The Bashful Bookworm tells the story of a girl that finds a friend through a mutual love of books. The film went on to win the award for Best Usage of Prop, Line, and Character Name, the second time I had won that particular award (previously won for Break). I used Premiere Pro and my DSLR camera to create The Bashful Bookworm. This is the second short film I have co-written and directed.
