Scrummer

Scrummer is an Agile issue tracking software created by my Software Engineering II course. Scrummer was built to act as a tool to be used by future classes to manage sprints and track issues. My class was separated into teams and allocated a portion of the application to take ownership of. My team was the TaskHistory team; we developed the TaskHistory components and communicated with other teams to integrate our work together. I played the role of scrum master and developer during our four sprints. We developed our components in vertical slices that encompassed both the front and backend. It was a great opportunity to work as an Agile team with peers to build a large-scale application from the ground up.

Tools Used:

  • SpringMVC
  • Java
  • Bootstrap
  • CSS
  • Hiberate
  • SQL
