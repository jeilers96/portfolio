The Film Auditor

Thefilmauditor.com is a movie blog that gives me a platform to discuss, critique, and rave about the films I see throughout the year. I created this blog to combine both of my passions: website design and film. This was a fun project to help improve my skills in front end design and development as well as my writing. I am still very active on this website. I try to post something, whether it is a movie review, list, or related article, twice a month.

While working on this project, I gained experience working with breakpoints and responsive design. I also learned to utilize HTML5 import elements to achieve my desired design for the website's homepage. I hope to continue working on this website by posting and tweaking the site to perform and look great.

Tools Used:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Javascript and JQuery
