Break

Break is a short film I wrote and directed for the 2017 UAC 48 Hour Film Project. "Antihero" was the theme of the competition; all filmmakers were given a prop, character name, and line that needed to be included in the film. At the premiere of competition, Break won the award for Best Usage of Prop, Line, and Character Name. Break was the first short film that I wrote and directed.
