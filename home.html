About Me

Hi! I am Josh Eilers, a senior Computer Science student at the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire. I am also a Software Engineering Intern at Great Lakes where I have been a part of over 30 Agile sprints, working on various web applications. In my free time, I enjoy working on projects that combine my two passions: web design and film.

I am eager to learn new things and expand my horizons to help improve the code I write. As a computer scientist, I am fueled by my desire to bring my own and others' ideas to life. I do this by using my resourcefulness, creativity, and communication skills to create a product that makes everyone happy.

When I am not programming, I enjoy watching and making movies, playing games with my friends, and being outdoors. Some of my recent accomplishments include making the Spring 2018 Dean's List, launching my own movie blog (thefilmauditor.com), and winning an award for my film The Bashful Bookworm.

If you want to find out more about me, please look around my website and peek at my resume. I am always looking for new challenges and adventures.
